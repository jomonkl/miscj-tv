About AeroTV

AeroTV is a premium, open-source web-based IPTV media player designed to watch free and legal public television channels from around the world.

Features:

  • Curated Playlists: Pre-loaded playlists categorized by News, Movies, Music, and Documentaries.
  • Custom Playlists: Input your own M3U or M3U8 IPTV URL.
  • Favorites System: Star your favorite channels for quick loading.
  • Custom Controls: Play/Pause, volume, stats panel, Picture-in-Picture, and Fullscreen modes.
  • CORS Proxy: Built-in support to stream files bypassing strict browser Cross-Origin Resource Sharing rules.
  • Cyberpunk & Glassmorphism themes: Switch styles seamlessly to match your aesthetics.

Important Notice Regarding Streams:

This program plays streams from publicly available playlists. Some streams may experience downtime, geo-restrictions, or require standard browser permissions. If a stream fails, try activating the "Load via Proxy" button or check if your internet connection is filtering HLS packets.
